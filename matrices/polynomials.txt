Polynomial

- A collection of terms where each term has a coefficient and exponent eg. 3x^2
- Can represent each term as a struct with coef and exponent
- Then polynomial can be an array of terms

Adding polynomials
- initialise an array where size is the sum of the size of 2 polynomials
- initialise i and j pointers for arr 1 and arr 2. initialise k for arr 3
- while i < arr1.length and j < arr2.length
    - if i exponent > j exponent: copy i into arr 3. increment i and k
    - if j exponent > i exponent: copy j into arr 3. increment j and k
    - if i exponent = j exponent: copy sum of i and j coefficients into arr 3. increment i, j, k
