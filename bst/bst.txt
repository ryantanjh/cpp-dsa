Binary search tree

- For each node, all elements on the left are smaller, all elements on the right are bigger
- Cannot have duplicates
- In-order traversal gives sorted order

Drawback of BST
- It is possible you generate a BST of height n eg. inserting 1,2,3,4,5,6,7
- Cannot control this as the inputs are user inputs
- That is why we need AVL trees, which is balanced BST
